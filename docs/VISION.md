# CarCareAI Vision

CarCareAI aims to become the smartest personal vehicle management application.

The long-term vision is to provide drivers with a complete digital assistant capable of tracking every aspect of vehicle ownership.

Future releases will include artificial intelligence, predictive maintenance, OCR-based odometer reading, cloud synchronization, analytics, and intelligent reminders.

The project prioritizes reliability, simplicity, offline-first operation, and a premium user experience.
